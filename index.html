BIM Consultancy

BIM Processes

As BD Architects, we provide services with a team specialised in BIM (Building Information Modelling) processes. BIM is an innovative method used in the planning, design, construction and management phases of projects. This process enables projects to be managed in a more efficient, sustainable and cost-effective manner. Thanks to BIM technologies, effective communication and cooperation between all project stakeholders is ensured, errors are minimised and quality is increased.
